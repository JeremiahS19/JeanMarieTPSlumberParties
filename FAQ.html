How much space do I need for the tents?

Generally a 12 x 12 space will give you plenty of room for a 5 person party. Depending on the configuration of the tent set-up, there needs to be adequate space for guests to access their tents.

CAN I add extra tents?

Yes! The charge to add a tent to any party is $25.00 per additional decorated tent.
